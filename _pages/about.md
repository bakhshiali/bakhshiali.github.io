Ali Bakhshi is a writer, inventor, ideator, innovator, researcher, technologist, programmer, lecturer, elite, and industrial designer in the fields of nanotechnology, nanobiotechnology, cold plasma, quantum technologies, and machine learning [<a href="https://en.everybodywiki.com/Ali_Bakhshi_(Researcher_and_Inventor)">1</a>]. He holds B.Sc. of engineering physics (Plasma, Laser, and Solid-state physics) and M.Sc. of nanotechnology-nanomaterials [<a href="https://en.everybodywiki.com/Ali_Bakhshi_(Researcher_and_Inventor)">1</a>]. He has several projects [<a href="https://bakhshiali.github.io/projects/">2</a>-<a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/ali-bakhshi-/">3</a>], ISI article [<a href="https://orcid.org/0000-0001-9825-0806">4</a>], English chapters/books [<a href="https://orcid.org/0000-0001-9825-0806">4</a>], translation of advanced scientific books, national and international patent [<a href="https://en.everybodywiki.com/Ali_Bakhshi_(Researcher_and_Inventor)">1</a>], two silver medals of the World Inventors Competition [<a href="https://en.everybodywiki.com/Ali_Bakhshi_(Researcher_and_Inventor)">1</a>], the top student of the master's degree [<a href="https://en.everybodywiki.com/Ali_Bakhshi_(Researcher_and_Inventor)">1</a>], refereeing articles of prestigious ISI magazines [<a href="https://orcid.org/0000-0001-9825-0806">4</a>], artificial intelligence [<a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=JHn_W_wAAAAJ&hl=en">5</a>], startups, and etc. in his resume.
He is an official member of the International Federation of Inventors Associations (IFIA) and received the title of INV in 2022 (also known as Inv. Ali Bakhshi) [<a href="https://www.ifia.com/inv/ir2022jan809acxb/">6</a>].
